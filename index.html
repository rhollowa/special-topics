About

The instructor

Russell Holloway is an Adjunct Professor in Business Studies at Austin Community College, Northridge Campus. He teaches BUSI 2305 Business Statistics and is co-developing the new Special Topics course in AI management for the Entrepreneurship AAS program. Outside the classroom, he is the founder of TourcasT, a live music discovery platform built on AI-managed operational tooling.

Email: russell.holloway@austincc.edu
